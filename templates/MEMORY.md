## Accounting Rules
- Positive balance = owner has credit (overpaid / prepaid)
- Negative balance = owner owes money
- Fee calls are DEBITS (reduce balance)
- Payments received are CREDITS (increase balance)
- Year-end closing replaces provisional quarterly charges with actual approved total
- Never delete ledger entries — corrections via Adjustment entries
- Private charges (legal fees, admin charges for individual units) tracked separately
